The Blue Bees Recovery

Our Organization

The Hands for Nature Project was created by Kamya and Aleesha Kumar, two students who strive to create an impactful difference on the globe.

The Hands for Nature Project supports a growing movement to benefit the natural world and encourage environmental awareness.

The nonprofit organisation was built on the belief that society can become a collective drive that can change the world.

Through this initiative, we are able to hold on to the roots of beauty the world has provided together, hand in hand.

Our Mission And Vision

Our Mission is to offer healing through beekeeping to drug addicts and their children who have tried and failure conventional rehabilitation paths. We offer an unsual yet intriguing healing practices - resulting in increased openness to rehab . The Blue Bees organization is a committed to supporting transformation and purpose for people living with an addiction as well as their children. The organization employs globally renowned beekeepers with years of caring and loving bees.

We aim to dimish the stigma against rehab and produce healed functional people who are ready to contribute to society.
