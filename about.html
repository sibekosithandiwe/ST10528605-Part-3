Welcome to Blue Bees Recovery

Blue Bees is a nonprofit organization built on the idea that healing can come through nature and community. Founded on World Bee Day, it uses beekeeping as a way to support rehabilitation, offering people a chance to rebuild their lives with purpose and hope. At its heart, Blue Bees is about renewal—helping individuals and families find strength, connection, and empowerment through the simple yet powerful work of caring for bees.

What We Offer

The organization at its core is an alternative to traditional rehabilitation centres for drug abuse.The organization offers a Recovery Program, Heal the Parent Heal the Child Program and Touring services.
